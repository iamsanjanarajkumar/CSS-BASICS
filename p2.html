There are 4 different combination in CSS..!!

  1. Descedant Selector (using SPACE)
  2. Child Selector (using > SYMBOL)
  3. Adjacent Sibling Selector (using + SYMBOL)
  4. General Sibling Selector(using ~ SYMBOL)

Descedant Selector(USING SPACE) Example...!!!

third Paragraph..(child Element)
